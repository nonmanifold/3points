Three points on the plane, a parallelogram and a circle.

Small demo that shows how HTML5 canvas might be used to create interactive web application. Demo allows creation and drawing of related shapes: a parallelogram and a circle.

Click to enter three points. Theirs positions will be used to create a blue parallelogram. Yellow circle will be placed at parallelogram's diagonals intersection, and will maintain the same area as the parallelogram.
Drag red handles to move three corner points around and notice, how shapes react to your input.
Click "Reset" to start over.

Application has several unit tests for its logic parts and was also tested for stability by a 5y.o. boy.
